Lamborghini Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to some of the most common questions about Lamborghini.

1. How much does a Lamborghini cost?

Most new Lamborghinis start around $200,000, with high-end models costing much more.

2. What makes Lamborghinis so fast?

They use powerful engines, lightweight materials, and aerodynamic designs.

3. Are Lamborghinis street legal?

Yes, all standard Lamborghini models are street legal.

4. How long does it take to build a Lamborghini?

Each car is hand assembled and takes several months to complete.

5. Do Lamborghinis hold their value?

Some models depreciate, but rare editions may increase in value.

6. How fast can a Lamborghini go?

Many models can exceed 200 mph.

7. Where are Lamborghinis made?

They are made in Sant'Agata Bolognese, Italy.

8. Can you daily drive a Lamborghini?

You can, but they are not very practical for everyday use.
